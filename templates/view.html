Sugared Banana

1 tsp Cinnamon
2 tsp Sugar
1 Banana

  1. Mix the Cinnamon and Sugar to create Cinnamon Sugar
  2. Slice the banana and spread slices on a plate
  3. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar over the siced banana & enjoy!
